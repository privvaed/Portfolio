About Me

I have a passion for capturing the beauty of everyday moments, people, and my adventures through the lens of my camera. Photography, for me, is more than just a hobby — it's a way of storytelling, preserving memories, and evoking emotions.

Besides photography, I also have a knack for web development. There's something magical about bringing ideas to life on the web, just as I do with my photographs. And of course, like any other teenager, I have a fondness for good food — who doesn't, right?

I won 3rd place in a photography contest hosted by The Hillside Echo, the student publication at Filamer Christian University. It was a validation of my passion and dedication to the art of photography. I was also given the opportunity to photograph the 119th Foundation Anniversary Celebration parade on November 13, 2023.
